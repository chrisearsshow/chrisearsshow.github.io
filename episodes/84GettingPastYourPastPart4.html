#84. Getting Past Your Past Part 4.

Chris: In the last chapter we covered exercises like Safe Calm Place, Breathing Shift and Butterfly Hug. We’ll be using them again in this chapter, as we start looking at unprocessed memories.

Voice Of Judgement: How do you find the ones that are unprocessed?

C: One way is to start with a recent event that’s bothering you. When you bring up that memory, notice what emotion comes with it. Do a body scan and see if it shows up somewhere in your body.

VOJ: But how do you know it’s unprocessed?

C: On a scale of 0-10, where 0 is neutral and 10 is the most disturbing, evaluate that memory. If it’s 3 or higher, it’s probably not fully processed.

VOJ: What?! That could be a lot of memories.

C: There are generally about 10 to 20 unprocessed memories that are responsible for most of the pain and suffering in most of our lives.

VOJ: We’re going to be in therapy forever!

C: I think the idea is that since all the memories are linked, you delve in to earlier and earlier memories. Eventually you’ll see what your negative beliefs are.

VOJ: All my beliefs are negative. Traffic is bad. Sugar is bad. Everything is bad.

C: It’s more about beliefs about yourself. They usually fall under the categories of responsibility (like I did something wrong), or lack of safety (like I can’t trust anyone) or lack of control and power (like I can’t get what I want).

VOJ: So what do you do with those memories and negative beliefs?

C: For right now, it’s enough to just keep a written log of them and after each memory, to remember to use Safe Calm Place and Breathing Shift.

VOJ: If we need a safe calm place, shouldn’t we just go to a library?

C: That could work.

VOJ: Do you how books stay warm in the libraries?

C: How?

VOJ: They wear book jackets. What is one life advice you can expect from a librarian?

C: Read a book?

VOJ: You need to believe in your shelf.

C: I can’t believe people listen to this podcast.

VOJ: A drunk man goes into a library and shouts “CAN I HAVE TWO CHEESEBURGERS AND SOME FRIES?” The librarian looks at him in shock and says “Sir, this is a library!” The man, embarrassed, apologetically lowers his voice and whispers “Sorry, can I have two cheeseburgers and some fries?”
